About Us

Welcome to Idaho Skiing, your ultimate guide to exploring the best ski resorts in Idaho! Whether you're an experienced skier, a beginner, or someone looking to enjoy a winter getaway, we’re here to provide you with all the information you need to make the most of your skiing adventure in the Gem State.

Our Mission

At Idaho Skiing, we’re dedicated to promoting the incredible skiing experiences that Idaho offers. From the majestic slopes of Sun Valley to the powdery terrains of Tamarack Resort, Brundage Mountain, and Bogus Basin, we aim to highlight why Idaho is quickly becoming one of the top ski destinations in the United States. Our mission is simple: to help you discover and experience the best that Idaho skiing has to offer, all while making your trip planning as seamless as possible.

Meet Susan Wright

Our editor and primary advocate for Idaho skiing, Susan Wright, is passionate about the sport and the incredible potential Idaho has for skiers of all levels. Based in Boise, Susan brings over a decade of experience in the skiing world, both as an avid skier and a dedicated writer. Her deep understanding of Idaho's ski resorts, combined with her love for winter sports, drives her to share the hidden gems of the state with visitors from around the world.

With Susan’s expertise, you can trust that every article and resource on Idaho Skiing has been carefully researched and curated to provide you with the most accurate, up-to-date information. Her goal is to help you find the best slopes, resorts, and accommodations, ensuring that your trip to Idaho is unforgettable.

Why Choose Idaho Skiing?

Idaho is fast gaining a reputation for its amazing snow conditions, stunning terrain, and welcoming atmosphere. Unlike the crowded resorts of larger ski destinations, Idaho offers an uncrowded, family-friendly environment that allows skiers to truly experience the natural beauty of the mountains.

At Idaho Skiing, we believe in providing accurate and insightful content, from resort reviews to skiing tips, so that you can plan your perfect winter getaway. Whether you're looking for expert advice on the best powder runs or need help finding affordable lift tickets, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Thank you for choosing Idaho Skiing as your resource for discovering all the incredible skiing Idaho has to offer. We hope to see you on the slopes soon!

For questions or more information, feel free to reach out to Susan Wright at susan@idaho-skiing.com.
