FAQ

What is the largest ski resort in Idaho?

The largest ski resort in Idaho is Sun Valley Resort.

How big is Sun Valley Resort?

Sun Valley Resort covers a vast area of over 2,000 acres.

What amenities does Sun Valley Resort offer?

Sun Valley Resort offers a range of amenities, including multiple ski slopes, snowboarding terrain, cross-country skiing trails, ski schools, rental equipment, restaurants, shops, and luxurious accommodations.

How many ski slopes are there at Sun Valley Resort?

Sun Valley Resort boasts 121 ski slopes, catering to all skill levels from beginners to advanced skiers.

Are there any specific attractions or features at Sun Valley Resort?

Yes, Sun Valley Resort features the famous Bald Mountain, known for its challenging and thrilling ski runs that attract experienced skiers from all over.

Does Sun Valley Resort offer activities other than skiing?

Absolutely! Sun Valley Resort offers a variety of activities, including ice skating, snowshoeing, sleigh rides, snowmobiling, spa facilities, and even a year-round outdoor heated swimming pool.

Is Sun Valley Resort suitable for beginners and families?

Yes, Sun Valley Resort warmly welcomes beginners and families with its dedicated beginner slopes, ski schools, and family-friendly amenities, making it an ideal destination for all ski enthusiasts.

How far is Sun Valley Resort from major cities in Idaho?

Sun Valley Resort is located approximately 150 miles northeast of Boise, the capital city of Idaho.

Are there any nearby accommodations for visitors to stay at?

Yes, Sun Valley Resort offers a range of accommodations, including hotels, lodges, condominiums, and vacation rentals, ensuring visitors can find the perfect place to stay for their ski trip.

Is Sun Valley Resort open year-round?

While Sun Valley Resort is primarily known for its winter activities, it also offers various summer activities, making it a year-round destination for outdoor enthusiasts.
